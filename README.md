# LegalEase
AI-Powered Bilingual Legal Assistant (Urdu + English)

LegalEase is an intelligent LegalTech platform designed to make legal understanding accessible, affordable, and user-friendly for individuals, freelancers, and small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs) in Pakistan.

🚀 Overview

LegalEase leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Large Language Models (LLMs) to simplify complex legal processes. It allows users to analyze contracts, generate legal documents, and understand legal content in plain language.

The system focuses on bridging the gap between legal complexity and everyday users.

✨ Key Features
📄 Document Analysis
Upload contracts (PDF/DOCX)
Detect risky clauses and missing terms
Highlight obligations and liabilities
🧾 Legal Document Generation
Generate NDAs, agreements, affidavits
Guided templates for ease of use
🧠 Smart Summarization
Converts complex legal text into simple explanations
Supports Urdu + English bilingual output
🎙️ Voice Interaction (Optional / Planned)
Ask legal questions via speech
Convert voice → text → AI response
⚠️ Risk Detection
Identifies:
Liability limitations
Arbitration clauses
Missing protections
Ambiguous wording
👨‍⚖️ Expert Referral
Connects users to verified legal professionals
Triggered when AI confidence is low
